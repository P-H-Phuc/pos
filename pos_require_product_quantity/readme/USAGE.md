To enable the feature, go to **Point of Sale** → **Configuration** →
**Settings**, select your POS, and enable **Require Product Quantity**
under the *Product Prices* section.

Once enabled, if a cashier clicks the **Payment** button while one or
more order lines have a quantity of 0, an alert dialog is shown listing
the affected products. The payment screen will not open until all
quantities are set to a non-zero value.
